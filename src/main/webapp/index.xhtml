About allo-doc

Allo-doc is a free service used to get an appointment with a doctor around you. Register and you can use it right now, simply go to the register page. Every functionality offered by Allo-doc is free.

Are you a certified doctor ? You can register too ! You can use Allo-doc so that people can get appointments with you. Just sign in and you will have a special account.
